Welcome to ICECE 2024

The 13th International Conference on Electrical and Computer Engineering (ICECE 2024) is the pioneering international conference in the areas of electrical and computer engineering. ICECE brings together individuals from academia, research organizations, government, and industry, to discuss and share ideas and present their latest findings and experiences in the areas of electrical and computer engineering. Prospective authors are invited to submit original technical papers for presentation at the conference and publication in the conference proceedings. Accepted and presented papers will be submitted for inclusion into IEEE Xplore subject to meeting IEEE Xplore's scope and quality requirements.
